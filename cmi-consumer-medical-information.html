Active ingredients

There are over 600 chemical compounds in the cannabis plant. The most well-known and researched active ingredients in the cannabis plant are the cannabinoids CBD and THC. Cannabis products are classified according to the concentration of these substances.

Other major known components are called Terpenoids, with separate action mechanisms that create synergy (known also as the Entourage Effect) with cannabinoids.

This combination is expressed in medical properties, such as: pain reduction, reduction in anxiety, anti-inflammatory activity, quality of sleep, and more.

Read the entire document carefully before using this product. If you have any questions, consult your healthcare professional.
